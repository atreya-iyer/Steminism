    Summer Camps 2016

    Below is a collection of summer camps for girls interested in STEM.

    The STEM Summer Institute is for rising 7th through 12th grade girls was created in 2011 as a model STEM summer program for girls. The aim for this camp is to model true integration of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics in a domain that is naturally appealing to girls.

    The Mt. Diablo GIRL Summit, C-STEM GIRL Camp is focused on motivating girls in middle school through peer mentoring, to learn computing and STEM concepts through a fun and exciting robotics-based curriculum.

    The TechTrek camps at Davis and Irvine are science and math camps designed to develop interest, excitement, and self-confidence in young women who will enter eighth grade in the fall.
